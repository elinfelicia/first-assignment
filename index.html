Tintin

This young man is Tintin. He is an adorable Imperial Shi-tzu who stays with us every other week.

He is not a fan of physical excercise, or staying awake for that matter. Most of the time you can find him exactly like this. Fast asleep.

He is, however, a big fan of attention. Which would result in most of my working days looking something like this.

The Fish

This is Frank and Frances. They are as beautiful as they are fertile and viciously mean to all the other fish in the tank.

They never stop having babies in a tank that does not have the capacity for that. They truly make me wish neutering fish was a thing one could do.

And this stunner here is Dennis (top) and his wife Denise (bottom). They are well-behaved, with no babies, and they used to rule the tank before Frank and Frances moved in.
